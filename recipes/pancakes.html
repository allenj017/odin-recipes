Protein Pancakes

Description

These protein pancakes are a quick and healthy breakfast option

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g of whey protein powder
  • 250g plain flour
  • 1tbsp of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Peel the banana and mash it in a large mixing bowl until smooth.
  2. Crack the eggs into the bowl and whisk together with the mashed banana until well combined.
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the whey protein powder and plain flour to the mixture.
