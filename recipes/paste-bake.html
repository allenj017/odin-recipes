Pasta Bake

Description

An easy and hearty meal that is perfect for family dinner or meal prep

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 100g grated cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until just tender, then drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil. Saute onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Pour the mixture into a baking dish. Sprinkle cheese evenly on top.
  5. Bake at 200 for 20-25 minutes.
